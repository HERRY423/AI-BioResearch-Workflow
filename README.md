# Overview

This repository represents my personal **AI-powered Computational Biology Research Infrastructure**.

The goal is to establish a systematic workflow integrating:

- Artificial Intelligence agents
- Biological knowledge reasoning
- Computational pipelines
- Reproducible research environments
- Scientific literature intelligence

for advanced research in:

- Single-cell RNA sequencing (scRNA-seq)
- Spatial transcriptomics (ST)
- Tumor immunology
- Rare cell identification
- Foundation models in biology
- Deep learning for biological discovery


The long-term vision is to build an:

> **AI-native Computational Biology Laboratory**

where human scientific intuition collaborates with AI research agents to accelerate biological discovery.

# Research Philosophy

Modern computational biology research requires integration of four fundamental capabilities:                                                                          Scientific Question
        |
        ↓
Biological Understanding
        |
        ↓
Computational Modeling
        |
        ↓
Experimental Validation


This repository follows the principle:

> AI should not replace scientific thinking.
> AI should amplify hypothesis generation, analysis capability, and research productivity.
